Giving

Thank you for your support of Greater Tabernacle Baptist Church and our mission to teach the faithful, reach the lost and care for all. We are very appreciative of all monetary donations. We are a 501(3)(c) organization and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Unless you designate otherwise, your gift will be used wherever it is most needed to assist our ministry in expanding our services.

For more information on donating to our church, feel free to attend one of our services, call 212.368.4754 or e-mail us at churchadmin@greatertabernaclebc.org

Want to donate now? Click the Donate button below.
Thank you!

We also depend on the generosity of our members and individuals in the community, like you, to provide items for people in need. Since 1954, we have been handing out clothes and feeding the hungry. More than half of those helped are families. We accept all non-perishable food items in unopened containers and new and gently used clothing and accessories.
